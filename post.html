This Chinese grandfather is breaking fashion stereotypes one step at a time. Literally. Wang Deshun is a 80-year-old actor and artist who recently made his first appearance on a podium as a model and absolutely crushed it.

80-Year-Old Grandpa Tries Modeling For The First Time And Totally Slays His Runway Debut

Posted by BoredPanda on 2016

Wang made his debut during China Fashion Week and quickly found himself in the national spotlight. Because of his charming charisma and fit body people started calling him “China’s hottest grandpa” and nobody could argue with them. “It’s about your state of mind. It’s not about age. Nature determines age, but you determine your state of mind,” says Wang. However, his attitude is only one side of the coin. The grandpa also says he works out about 3 hours per day.
